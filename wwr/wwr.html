Teran Whitewater Rafting Company

Mission: To provide exhilarating and safe whitewater rafting adventures that connect our guests with nature, create unforgettable memories, and inspire a lifelong passion for outdoor exploration. Vision: To be the leading adventure tourism provider, recognized globally for sustainable practices, exceptional customer experiences, and fostering environmental stewardship through thrilling water expeditions. Motto: "Ride the Rapids, Embrace the Adventure!" Creed: We believe in the power of adventure to transform lives, the responsibility to protect the rivers we cherish, and the joy of sharing the wild beauty of nature with everyone who dares to paddle with us.

History

Founded in 2010 in Cochabamba, Bolivia, our company began as a dream to share the thrill of whitewater rafting with locals and visitors alike. Inspired by the majestic rivers of the Andean region, we started with just a small team of passionate outdoor enthusiasts and one raft. Over the years, we’ve grown into a trusted adventure provider, offering guided rafting trips that showcase the stunning landscapes of Bolivia, from the Rocha River to the Chapare lowlands. Our commitment to safety, sustainability, and exceptional experiences has made us a favorite among adventure seekers. Today, we are proud to be a pioneer in eco-tourism in Cochabamba, introducing thousands to the beauty of Bolivia’s rivers while promoting their conservation.
