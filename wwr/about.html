Adventure Co.

Our purpose is to connect adventure lovers with the energy of nature, acting as your guide to the wild outdoors. Our mission is to provide safe and exciting rafting trips that challenge you physically while teaching you how to protect the environment. We follow the creed that the river brings people together through teamwork and respect, believing that life is much more fun when you leave the shore behind. Guided by our motto, "Ride the Current, Respect the River," we promise to turn every rapid into a success and every journey into an unforgettable memory.

History

Our company, Adventure Co., was founded in 1998 by Jack Thompson, a former professional kayaker. After retiring from competition, Jack realized he missed the water. He wanted to share the excitement of the river with ordinary people, not just athletes. In the beginning, the company was very small. Jack worked alone out of a small wooden shed near the riverbank, with just two old rafts and a used van. He acted as the guide, the driver, and the manager all at the same time.Despite the hard work, his enthusiasm was contagious, and customers kept coming back, bringing their friends and families with them. By 2010, the business had grown significantly. Jack’s daughter, Emily, joined the team to help modernize the company. She introduced online booking systems and added new, safer equipment, but she insisted on keeping the personal touch that her father was famous for. Today, we are proud to be the leading rafting provider in the region. We have a team of 15 certified guides and a fleet of modern boats. While we have expanded over the years, we are still a family business at heart, dedicated to safety, fun, and the great outdoors.
